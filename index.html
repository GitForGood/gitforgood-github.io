Goblin raiders

A deckbuilding board game where the player balances being a menace to their surroundings and keeping their ragtag band of goblins in check long enough to secure a foothold in the region.

Cauldron Chaos

A cooperative boardgame where the players playing as critters cooperate to escape a witch's hut before the witch manages to capture them all.

Homescape

A platform for configuring IOT devices in a smart home that is accessible to outsiders by utilizing a digital twin of the home.

It allows users to orient themselves in real space and use their knowledge of the layout of the home to configure their smart home to work for them independantly of who initially set up the devices.
